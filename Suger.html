Want is Sugar?

Sugar is a soluble carbohydrate that is made out of three elements, carbon, hydrogen and oxygen. They are found in most plant tissues, most commonly in sugarcane and sugar beet. The world has 170.81 million tons of sugar between 2016/17, Brazil is the largest producer of sugar due to having the largest amount of sugarcane.

The Histroy

Back in the ancient time people chew raw sugarcane to extract its sweetness and used honey to for sweetening in most part of the world. Sugar was found in the 1st century CE by Europe but it was only used for medical purposes, this continued until the 5th century where Indians discovered how to turn sugarcane juice into crystals called khanda (candy). In the 7th century Chinese Emperor, Tang Taizong has made it know that he was interest in sugar. This allowed china to have it first sugarcane plantations.
